I grew up in Arlington, Vermont, a bucolic setting that went unappreciated until I spent a few years surrounded by Worcester's dilapidation. I was educated by the faculty at Fisher Elementary and Arlington High Schoo, but school work was an afterthought. Between the ages of 10-18 I devoted all interest and effort to soccer, basketball, and baseball.

I have 3 siblings: one older brother, JJ; one older sister, Carol; and one younger brother, Conor. JJ is six years older, Carol is four. Conor is a little more than two years younger. Given the age differences, much of JJ's and Carol's pursuits were unrelatable, while Conor and I overlapped on most things.
